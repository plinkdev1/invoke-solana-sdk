This Privacy Policy describes how INVOKE SDK ("we", "us") handles information when you visit invoke-landing.netlify.app ("Site"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

This Site is hosted on Netlify. Netlify may collect standard server logs including IP addresses and browser information as part of their infrastructure operations. Please refer to Netlify's Privacy Policy for details.

5. SDK Usage

The INVOKE SDK itself is open-source software that runs locally on the user's Android device. It does not transmit any data to our servers. All wallet interactions occur directly between the user's app and their chosen wallet application via the Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter protocol.
